Incident Overview (Known at Time of Briefing)
Public Safety Questions Complete as soon as practical — priority above all else
1
Is there an outstanding suspect? Where are they and what is their description? Lying in ambush? How long ago did they flee? What weapons are they armed with?
2
Was the officer or anyone else injured that may need assistance?
3
Where is the crime scene? May be apparent but maybe not. Where was the initial contact and are there other locations?
4
Did the officer fire their weapon? If shots were fired, which direction were they fired?
5
Who are the witnesses? Identify and separate as quickly as possible — evidence and people must be gathered promptly.
